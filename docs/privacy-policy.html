Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Kebab Locator ("the App") is developed by Lucas. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Location: The App requests access to your device's precise location to show kebab restaurants near you. Location data is used only on-device to perform searches and is not stored on our servers.

Submitted content: If you voluntarily submit a kebab shop (name, address, notes), that information is stored in our database to improve the community map.

Advertising identifiers: The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking in your device Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To find and display kebab restaurants near your location
  • To save your favorites locally on your device
  • To display non-intrusive advertisements that support free access to the App

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database backend for kebab shop listings (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — map data, no personal data sent

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as described above (AdMob, Supabase) or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

Location data is never stored by us. Submitted shop data is kept indefinitely to maintain the community database. You may request deletion by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
